BE ON TIME

Always check out the announcement that made by teacher and take note of the exam
date and time. The exam will be available on time, within the duration set by the teacher.

ONLY ONE ATTEMPT

Student can only take the exam once, within the time set by the teacher. When student click the 'Start Exam' button, the student is considered already sit for the exam. If a student miss an exam, teacher can set another date
and time for the same exam and only students who miss the exam can take the re-open
exam.

STABLE INTERNET CONNECTION

If your internet connection is disconnected suddenly, do not close the browser, refresh the question page when your device re-connect to the internet. If the exam is still available, you can still continue save answers.
However, if the exam time is expired, then you cannot answer the question anymore.

DO NOT CLOSE BROWSER

Do not Close the browser if you have not yet save your Answer Closing the browser means
exit the exam. If you did not click the Save button to save the answer for every question and close the browser, you are not answering the questions and will get zero mark.

SAVE YOUR ANSWER

Remember to click the Save button to save the answer for every question. Even you did not
click 'Finish & Exit' button when you finish all questions, you still answered the questions
because your answers already saved.
